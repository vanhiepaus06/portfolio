Dino 21, Hanoi, Vietnam (Apr 2019 - Sep 2019)

Geargag is an e-commerce platform that provides fulfillment services for Print on Demand sellers, offering over 100 printable products that can be sold globally. The platform integrates with major selling platforms like Shopify, WooCommerce, Etsy, and Amazon to help sellers fulfill orders and ship worldwide.

As a Research and Development Specialist, I researched all printable products to identify the most promising ones and made recommendations to sellers.

I also conducted in-depth research on selling platforms and marketplaces, such as Shopify, Etsy, Amazon, and WooCommerce, to suggest functionalities for improving the Geargag platform.
